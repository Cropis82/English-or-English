Group 5: Human Security & Personal Agency (Philosophy)

1. The Paradigm Shift: Human vs. State Security

The core of the Senzatomica philosophy lies in redefining what it means to be "safe." Traditionally, security is seen through the lens of the State—borders, weapons, and military deterrence. Human Security flips this script.

State Security

Focuses on territorial integrity, nuclear deterrence, and military strength. It often assumes that "more weapons = more safety."

Human Security

Focuses on the individual. It prioritizes freedom from fear and freedom from want—addressing health, environment, and dignity.

2. Ordinary People & Global Politics

How can one person in a crowd change a nuclear policy? Senzatomica suggests that global politics is not an exclusive club for "experts."

  • Breaking the Myth of Powerlessness: By educating ourselves, we transform from passive observers into "protagonists" of history.
  • Grassroots Influence: Civil society movements (like the one that led to the TPNW) prove that when enough individuals change their mindset, governments eventually follow.
  • The Ripple Effect: A change in one person's spirit influences their family, their community, and eventually the global consensus.

3. Passive Peace vs. Active Peace

Peace is not merely the interval between two wars. It is a creative process.

Passive Peace: The "absence of conflict." It is fragile because the underlying tensions and nuclear threats still exist.
Active Peace: The "creation of value." It involves dialogue, the courage to understand the "other," and the constant effort to build bridges where walls once stood.

4. Applying the Message in Grugliasco/Turin

The "Global" starts "Local." Here is how we bring the Senzatomica spirit to our streets:

  • Dialogue in the Piazza: Replacing digital echo chambers with real, empathetic conversations in our local neighborhoods.
  • School Initiatives: Promoting workshops in Grugliasco schools that focus on non-violent conflict resolution.
  • Civil Society: Supporting local grassroots movements that prioritize environmental and social dignity in the Turin metropolitan area.
